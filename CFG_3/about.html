Easy Kitchen

About us

At the end of 2021, a couple of stressed Software Developer students decided to share online their favorite leftovers-based recipes that made them enjoy food with the little spare time they had. We know that work, study, and dealing with tight deadlines are not easy, so at least cooking should be! Therefore, we created Easy Kitchen to inspire people that do not have time to follow complicated recipes.

Our purpose

Easy Kitchen is a waste-conscious platform created with the purpose of making the most of leftovers in a way that everyone can cook. Food waste contributes to several problems like hunger, climate change, and economic concerns.

Using our free platform is as easy as opening your fridge: introduce the ingredient or ingredients you have left, and our unique search engine will show you what recipes we have that include your leftovers. You can choose any recipe from our continuously growing recipe database filled with amazing recipes from any part of the world. Stay alert for our seasonal specials, where you can find festive recipes perfect to make after the celebrations.
